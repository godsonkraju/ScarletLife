Welcome to the help page! Hopefully the deatils included here will be able to clear up any confusion!

Navigation

There are three main sections for navigation, the navigation bar, the home screen, and the community page.


Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is the header at the top of the page, it contains three buttons. The "Home" button will bring you to the home screen, the "About" button will bring you to a page where you can read about the story behind scarlet life, and you can log in to your google account by clicking the dropdown menu that says "Welcome, (user)!" Clicking this dropdown also gives you the optoin to view this page.


Home Screen

The home screen has two buttons. The "PLAY" button sends you to the level select screen, where you can select a campaign level to play. (See "Game Controls" for more information) The "GET CREATIVE!" button sends you to the creative community, where you can play levels that other people have published, and where you can create and publish your own levels as well!


Community Page

The community page has four tabs for navigation. From left to right: The "Create" Tab sends you to a level builder where you can create and edit your own custom level. (See "Level Builder Controls" for more information). The "Explore" tab opens up a level browser to play other people's levels. The "Favorites" tab opens up a level browser


Cell Types

Color Name Effect
Scarlet Life Hurst the player when they touch it.
Finish Touch a finish cell to beat a level!
Good Life Does not hurt the player, interupts Scarlet Life.
Player Ship The player's ship, this moves with game controls.
Keys THe finish unlocks once the player picks up all of these.
Void (Walls) Neither the plater, nor Scarlet Life, nor Good Life, can pass through this barrier.
Teleporters Touch a teleporter cell to get teleported to it's partner teleporter!
Blank (Dead) There is nothing here, just the blank vastness of space.
Bullets Travel across the screen when shot, they blow up when they collide with either Good or Scarlet life.
Powerups:
Outline Name Effect
Random Gives the player a random effect from below.
Health Restores the player's health.
Fuel Restores the player's fuel.
Slow Time Slows down time to 5 seconds.
Bullets Restores the player's bullets.
